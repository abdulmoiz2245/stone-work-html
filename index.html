About us

Stoneworks construction and Trading L.L.C is a resultant of the forward integration strategy taken up by the group to cater the various significant construction specialized services like maintenance, modification, renovation, rectification, interior finishes works etc, for our clients in building & infrastructure sector.

Our product combines unique materials, emulsions (bitumen), and aggregates. These materials are chosen with great care and according to international specifications for producing cold asphalt of all kinds for use in road maintenance and applied in various weather conditions. The cold mix asphalt is the right choice as it is environmentally friendly and easy Usage and conformity with international standards.
